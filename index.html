Natomas Village Apartments is a beautiful gated community located in the heart of Natomas, California just off of the I-5 and I-80. With convenience in mind, we are right across from Save Mart, and just a 5-minute drive from the Natomas Marketplace that includes Walmart, Best Buy, Home Depot, Regal Cinemas, and an assortment of restaurants. We are a 10-15 minute drive from Downtown and Midtown Sacramento.

Our community features spacious 1 and 2 bedroom apartments with wonderful amenities including:

- Gas, water, sewer, and garbage included

- Spacious balconies/patios

- Gated entry

- Courtesy patrol

- 24-hour fitness center

- 4 on-site laundry facilities
